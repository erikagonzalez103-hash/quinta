About

She teaches what she built first.

“For as long as I can remember, I’ve wanted to build something that helps women — not just to start a business they love, but to truly thrive at it, and to skip the expensive, lonely mistakes I made simply because I didn’t know what I didn’t know. Quinta & Co. is that something.” — Erika

Quinta & Co. is a practical business-and-AI education studio for women founders, created by Erika Gonzalez Harrison.

Erika co-founded Eridion Glass, LLC back in 2020 — and for years she ran it the way most of us do: on spreadsheets, manual social media, and a bookkeeping system held together by hope and a dozen browser tabs.

This year, she rebuilt how it runs. She hasn't opened a spreadsheet in months. Her bookkeeper and accountant are happy, the invoices go out on time, and the busywork that used to eat her evenings now mostly runs in the background. The time it gave back allowed her to build a whole second company — this one.

That's the whole idea.
She teaches what she built first.

And here's the honest part: she's not a guru on a mountaintop — she's a fellow founder, just a few steps ahead, who figured this out hands-on while running her own business. That's exactly why there's no jargon to wade through. Quinta is the practical version: The Foundations, the business fundamentals every owner needs, and The Practice, building real tools with AI. No lectures, no gatekeeping — just the things she wishes someone had walked her through, taught the way a friend would.

And she's not doing it alone. The Foundations are taught by a bench of vetted professionals — all women who are founders themselves. So every class comes from someone who does this work every day and has built a business of her own.
