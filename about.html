About

She teaches what she built first.

Quinta & Co. is a practical business-education studio for women founders, created by Erika Gonzalez Harrison.

Before Quinta, there was Eridion Glass — the company Erika co-founded. She built its entire operating system herself, with the same plain, low-cost tools she now teaches: no engineering team, no expensive software, no jargon. The bookkeeping, the contracts, the trackers, the AI assistant that runs quietly in the background — she made all of it work before she ever taught a class.

That's the whole idea. She teaches what she built first.

After months of quietly rebuilding her own workflow with Claude, she started teaching the women she loves the same things — over coffee, one hour at a time. The classes you'll find here are those lessons, made for the room: The Foundations, the business fundamentals every owner needs, and The Practice, building real tools with Claude. No lectures, no gatekeeping — just the things she wishes someone had shown her, taught the way a friend would teach them.
