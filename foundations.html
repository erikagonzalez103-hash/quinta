About The Foundations

When does enrollment open?

Fall 2026. The waitlist is open now — waitlisters hear first, before anything is announced publicly.

2

Who teaches these classes?

Women founders who do this work for a living — the bookkeeper teaches bookkeeping, the founder who's hired teaches hiring. Practitioners, not gurus.

3

Do I have to take all thirteen classes?

No. Take the one you need this month, or walk the whole path phase by phase. Each class stands on its own and ends with something you walk out holding.

No — every class is educational. You'll leave knowing what questions to ask and how the pieces work, but for decisions about your specific situation, talk to a qualified professional.

What can I do while I wait?
