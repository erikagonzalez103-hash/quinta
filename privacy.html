Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what information Quinta & Co. ("we," "us") collects when you visit quintaand.co, sign up for a class, or contact us — and what we do with it. We keep this simple, because that's how we do everything.

What we collect

  • Information you give us — your name and email when you sign up, book a class, or reach out.
  • Booking & payment details — handled by our scheduling and payment providers (see below). We don't store your card numbers.
  • Basic usage information — standard things your browser sends (like general location and pages visited) so the site works and we can improve it.

How we use it

  • To run classes and send you what you signed up for.
  • To process your bookings and payments.
  • To reply to you, and — if you opt in — to send occasional updates. You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • To keep the site working and make it better.

Who we share it with

We don't sell your information. We use a few trusted services to run the business, and your information passes through them only as needed:

  • Cal.com — class scheduling and bookings.
  • Stripe — secure payment processing.
  • Supabase — secure storage for sign-up information.
  • Google Fonts — loads the site's fonts (your browser's IP address is shared with Google to do this).

Each of these has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data.

Cookies

We keep tracking to a minimum. Some of the services above may set their own cookies to do their job (for example, to keep you logged in while booking).

Your choices

You can ask to see, correct, or delete the information we hold about you, and you can unsubscribe from emails at any time. Just email hello@quintaand.co.

Keeping your information safe

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of storage or transmission is ever 100% secure.

Children

This site and our classes are intended for adults and are not directed to children.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll change the date at the top. Please check back from time to time.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email hello@quintaand.co.
