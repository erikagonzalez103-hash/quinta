Why I started writing this

I started Eridion in 2020 on spreadsheets and manual everything — invoices I chased, posts I scheduled by hand, a bookkeeping system held together by hope and twelve browser tabs.

This year, I rebuilt how it runs. I haven't opened a spreadsheet in months. My bookkeeper and accountant are happy. The work that used to eat my evenings now mostly runs in the background. And the time it gave me back? It built a whole second company — this one.

I'm not years ahead of you. I'm a few months ahead. That's exactly why I think I can help. The tools that made this possible only got good recently, which means there's no decade of jargon to wade through — just the practical version of what actually works.

So this is where I'll write it down: the business fundamentals every owner needs, and the AI workflows that gave me my time back. Plain language, no gatekeeping, the things I wish someone had handed me.

If any of it is useful, come build something with me — the first hour is free.
