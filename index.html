Do I need any tech or AI experience?

None at all. Every class is plain-language and hands-on — no coding, no jargon. If you can use your phone, you can do this.

2

Who are these classes for?

Women founders at any stage — from just starting out to running an established business — who want practical skills without the overwhelm.

3

Where are classes held?

Small live classes in Dallas, plus online sessions you can join from anywhere.

4

What is Coffee with Quinta?

A free, 60-minute session where you build one small, working tool with AI — start to finish, right on your phone. It's the easiest way to see what's possible.

5

What will I walk out with?

Something real every time — a tool, a template, or a system you actually keep and use in your business.
