Is This Right For You?

A small private hour is intimate, paid, and built around real engagement. Read both lists honestly before continuing.

This Is For You If
  • You have an idea, project, or workflow you've wished you could build but couldn't justify hiring a developer for
  • You're curious about AI but want a guided, peaceful introduction — not another YouTube rabbit hole
  • You're willing to actively try things, ask questions, and laugh when something doesn't work the first time
  • You can give the hour your full attention — no half-listening while answering email
  • You're at a point in your business or life where doing more yourself feels like freedom, not failure
This Isn't For You If
  • You're hoping to leave with a finished, deployed app — that takes longer than an hour
  • You're already a working developer or have built apps yourself before — this will feel slow
  • You want to silently observe — the hour is hands-on and we build together
  • You're skeptical that AI tools have value and want to be convinced before engaging — fair, but a different kind of conversation
  • You're hoping for a free preview — this is an investment in yourself, and it asks one in return
